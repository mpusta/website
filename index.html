Hi, I'm Martin Pustavrh.

This is a collection of notes from a wandering mind. I write code to model financial markets, write fiction to make sense of everything else, and spend a lot of time thinking about what happens when AI reshapes how economies work. These interests don't always overlap, but when they do, that's usually where the interesting stuff lives.

Nothing here has to be practical or useful. It's a space to reflect, to follow whatever thread catches my attention, and to figure things out in the open.

What I'm exploring

Quantitative Investment Research
Building models, writing algorithms, and trying to find signal in noisy markets. I think in Python and think about risk, pricing, and the places where math meets real money.
The Economy of AI
What happens to labor markets, decision-making, and economic intuition when machines get good at pattern recognition? I dig into the research and try to write about it clearly.
Creative Writing
Stories, essays, and whatever falls in between. Writing is how I process the world when Python isn't enough.
